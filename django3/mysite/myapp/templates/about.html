About Us

Welcome to the Cuisine Aggregator!

We present you a website that allows you to explore our work. The purpose of the Cuisine Aggregator is to allow you to interact with data for cuisines, in the top 50 (and more) cities in the US. Special thanks to Yelp, from which most of our data is pulled. They were generous enough to give us access to the Yelp v3 API.


Here is a brief rundown of what you can explore on our website:

- Overview: get summary statistics and visualizations for data across all of the cities in our database

- Find Top Cuisines: get summary data and visualizations on best or worst cuisines in a city, with some possible inputs you can specify.

- City Snapshot: get summary graphs and facts on the food scene in a given city with customized specifications.

- Comparing Two Cities (Upcoming): select two cities and see how their cuisine data matches up through statistics and visualizations.

- Cuisine Lookup: get top cities for inputed cuisine.
