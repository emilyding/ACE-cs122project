An “oasis of civility” for “grown-ups”, Roland Liccioni’s Streeterville “institution” offers “exceptional” French haute cuisine “without trendy gimmicks” complemented by a “superb wine list” and “white-glove service”; an “exquisite townhouse” setting with “luxurious Parisian decor” and floral arrangements adds to the reasons it’s perfect for a “celebration” – or just a “vacation from the present” that “justifies the cost”; P.S. jackets suggested.
